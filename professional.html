Professional Life

For two summers I have worked for an interior design firm in my hometown. The name of the company is O'Brien and Muse and I worked as a summer intern. I helped out with office tasks including filing, organizing, speaking with various vendors and companies, and running errands among other things. The job has taught me a lot about the creative processes of interior designers as well as how things run behind the scenes. I am grateful for the experience I've gotten because it has taught me a lot about communication in a workspace as well as given me skills I hope to apply to future jobs. I was also kept company by the office cat, Fig, who made the work experience that much more enjoyable.
